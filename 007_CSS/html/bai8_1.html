Briards

A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog trditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards ios slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owned briards.
